# Privacy Policy – VoidFall

**Last updated: July 2026**

Thank you for playing **VoidFall**.

This Privacy Policy explains how VoidFall handles user data across all platforms, including Google Play, Steam, Epic Games Store, and any other official distribution platforms.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

VoidFall does not collect, store, or sell any personal information directly.

The game stores save data locally on your device. If cloud save features are enabled through your platform (such as Steam Cloud or Epic Games Services), save data may also be synchronized according to the platform provider's policies.

---

## 2. Online Features

VoidFall may use platform-provided online services to enable features such as:

- Multiplayer functionality
- Authentication
- Matchmaking
- Friends systems
- Achievements
- Leaderboards
- Cloud saves

These services may include:

- Epic Online Services (EOS)
- Steamworks services
- Google Play Games Services

Any data processed by these services is handled according to the respective platform's own privacy policies.

---

## 3. Advertising

VoidFall does not display any advertisements.

---

## 4. Purchases

VoidFall does not collect or store any payment information.

All purchases are processed securely by the platform through which you obtained the game (Google Play, Steam, or Epic Games Store). VoidFall does not have access to your payment details.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

VoidFall does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

However, platform services (such as Epic Online Services, Steamworks, or Google Play Games Services) may process limited data required to provide online features. This processing is governed by their respective privacy policies.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

VoidFall is not specifically directed toward children under the minimum age required by the platforms where it is distributed (Google Play, Steam, Epic Games Store, or others).

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

---

## 7. External Services

VoidFall may rely on third-party platform services to provide online functionality. These include, but are not limited to:

- Epic Online Services
- Steamworks
- Google Play Games Services

Each of these services has its own privacy policy, which governs how data is handled by them.

---

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be be posted within this document with an updated **"Last updated"** date.
