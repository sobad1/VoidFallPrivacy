# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 30, 2026

Thank you for playing **VoidFall**.

This Privacy Policy explains how VoidFall handles user data.

## Information We Collect

VoidFall does not collect, store, or sell personal information directly.

The game stores save data locally on your device.

## Online Features

VoidFall offers optional online multiplayer features through Epic Online Services (EOS).

When using these online features, Epic Online Services may process information necessary to provide authentication, matchmaking, friends, and multiplayer services. This data is processed according to Epic Games' own Privacy Policy.

Google Play Games Services may also be used to provide achievements and other Google Play features. Any information processed by Google Play Games Services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

## Advertising

VoidFall does not display advertisements.

## Purchases

VoidFall does not collect payment information directly. Any purchases made through Google Play are processed securely by Google.

## Data Sharing

VoidFall does not sell or share personal information with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

VoidFall is not specifically directed toward children under the applicable minimum age required by Google Play.
